rules/TOS

Be Kind and Respectful: Treat all members with kindness and respect. No Bullying or Harassment: Any form of harassment or personal attacks will result in your posts being deleted. Respect Different Opinions: People have different favorite characters and teams. Accept others' opinions even if you disagree. Do Not Use Bad Language: Keep the language clean. Swearing and offensive words are not allowed. No Impersonation: Do not pretend to be another member, a moderator, or any of the BFDI creators. Content and Posting Rules Stay On-Topic: All posts and discussions should be related to BFDI or other relevant Object Shows. No Inappropriate Content (NSFW): Posting any adult, violent, or graphic content is strictly forbidden. Credit the Artist: If you post art you didn't make, please credit the original artist. Do Not Post Personal Information: Keep yourself and others safe. Don't share addresses, phone numbers, etc. No Spoilers Without a Warning: When discussing a new episode, use a clear "Spoiler" tag in the title. Spam and Promotion Rules No Spamming: Do not post the same message or image over and over. No Excessive Self-Promotion: You can share your BFDI-related project once, but do not advertise it repeatedly. No Harmful Links: Do not post links to scam websites or malicious software. Moderation Rules (Manual Version) Use the Correct Sections: Post your topic in the right category (e.g., art in the art section). Do Not Troll: Don't intentionally start arguments or try to make other members angry. Do Not Encourage Rule-Breaking. To Report an Issue, Contact the Moderator: If you see someone breaking the rules, send an email to the address on the "Contact Us" page. Do not argue with the rule-breaker yourself. Respect the Moderator's Decisions: The moderator is here to keep the community safe. Their decisions are final. Consequences for Breaking Rules: Breaking the rules may lead to your posts being deleted. If you continue to break the rules, all of your contributions will be removed. Have Fun! The main goal is to have fun and share our love for BFDI
